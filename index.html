FAQs:

Which organization receive
feedback on Rateuz?

Rateuz specialized in obtaining feedback for two types of organisations:

  • Organisations which have registered with Rateuz
  • Organisations which a customer/client has requested to send their feedback How

How do organisatiions read their feedback?

Organisation will login to Rateuz, and view your feedback which is categorized in terms of quality. The organization have the option to respond to your feedback by leaving a comment on their feedback action page or to you directly.

What kind of organisations use
Rateuz?

Organisation from all sectors can use Rateuz, and this includes: restaurants and hospitality, healthcare, retail, legal, accounting, consultancy services, gyms, online services and ecommerce etc
